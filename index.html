The CMA program aims to enhance the skills and knowledge of financial professionals in areas such as financial planning, analysis, control, decision support, and professional ethics. It is awarded by the Institute of Management Accountants (IMA) in the United States.

The CMA program focuses on financial planning, analysis, control, decision support, and professional ethics.
